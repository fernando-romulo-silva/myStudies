Which of the following editions are offered by Docker?

A)
Docker Console Edition & Docker Enterprise Edition
 
B) 
Docker Community Edition & Docker Extended Edition

C) 
Docker Community Edition & Docker Enterprise Edition

D) 
Docker Container Edition and Docker Enhanced Edition  





































Answer C

Docker is available in two editions:

Docker Community Edition (CE) is ideal for individual developers and 
small teams looking to get started with Docker and experimenting with container-based apps.

Docker Enterprise Edition (EE) is designed for enterprise development and IT teams who build, 
ship, and run business critical applications in production at scale.
