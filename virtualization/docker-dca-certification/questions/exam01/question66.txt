
A user cannot push images to our DTR internal registry. 
What should we verify?


a) Docker login access.

b) That the DTR CA certificate should be trusted.

c) We should verify whether the image's repository does exist.

d) That the image has vulnerabilities and DTR's image scanning rejects the user's image.








































66 - Answer(s) 'a', 'b', and 'c'

Docker Trusted Registry (DTR), a highly available registry with secure image management capabilities
