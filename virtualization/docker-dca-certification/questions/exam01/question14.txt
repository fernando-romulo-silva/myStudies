
What is true about Swarm networking?

a) All overlay networks are encrypted by default.

b) Control Plane nodes use mutual TLS encryption to secure traffic.

c) An internal DNS can be consumed externally exposing its service.

d) All of the preceding options are true.








































Answer 'b'

The nodes in a swarm use mutual Transport Layer Security (TLS) to authenticate, authorize, and encrypt the communications with other nodes in the swarm.
