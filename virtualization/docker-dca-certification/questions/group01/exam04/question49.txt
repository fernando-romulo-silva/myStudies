Which 'docker service update' flags are used to update the published 
ports of existing services? (select two)

​A)​
-port-remove
​
B)
--publish-add
​
C)
--port-add
​
D)
--publish-rm









































The correct option are 'B' and 'D'

Update a service

--publish-add => Add or update a published port

--publish-rm  => Remove a published port by its target port
