Which flag of 'docker service create' and 'docker service update' configures 
the time delay between updates to a service task or sets of tasks?

a) ​-update-wait
​
b) --update-pause
​
c) --update-delay
​
d) --wait-delay












































Answer 'C'

The --update-delay flag configures the time delay between updates to a service task or sets of tasks. 

You can describe the time T as a combination of the number of seconds Ts, minutes Tm, or hours Th. So 10m30s indicates a 10 minute 30 second delay.

$ docker service create \
  --replicas 3 \
  --name redis \
  --update-delay 10s \
  redis:3.0.6
