Which of the following is NOT true about secrets?

a) Secrets are mounted in containers file system directly
​

b) Secrets can be used for storing username and password
​

c) Secrets are available at swarm services and standalone containers
​

d) Secrets are encrypted during transit and also at rest









































Answer 'a'

In terms of Docker Swarm services, a secret is a blob of data, such as a password, SSH private key, SSL certificate, 
or another piece of data that should not be transmitted over a network or stored unencrypted in a Dockerfile or in your application’s source code.
