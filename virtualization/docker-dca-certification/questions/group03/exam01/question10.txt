You are running UCP service on a swarm with 3 managers and 2 workers. 
You need to schedule a backup policy for your UCP service. 
How many nodes you need to fully back up this service?

A)
2

B)
1

C)
3

D)
5



































Answer B

Option B is correct because backing up data from 1 manager node is enough since all managers store the same data.
