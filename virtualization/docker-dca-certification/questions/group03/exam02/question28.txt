What is the default location of secrets inside a Docker container?

A)
/run/secrets/

B)
/secrets/

C)
/var/run/

D)
/var/secrets/





































Answer A

The location of the mount point within the container defaults to /run/secrets/<secret_name> in Linux containers, 
or C:\ProgramData\Docker\secrets in Windows containers, but you can also specify a custom location.
