_____________ is a Health Indicator Status provided in Spring Boot Actuator
  ​
A)
UNRESPONSIVE
​
B)
UNKNOWN
​
C)
DOWN

D)​
UP
   








































Answers 'B', 'C' and 'D'

UP, DOWN and UNKNOWN are Health Indicator Statuses provided and used by Spring Boot Actuator by default. 

https://docs.spring.io/spring-boot/docs/2.0.6.RELEASE/api/org/springframework/boot/actuate/health/Status.html
