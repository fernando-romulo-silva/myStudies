@RestController, @RequestBody and @ResponseBody are included in the _______ jar dependency.
  ​
A)
spring-web
​
B)
spring-mvc
​
C)
spring-web-mvc
​
D)
spring-core
   








































Answers 'A'

@RestController, @RequestBody, @ResponseBody are present in the spring-web module 
that has the Maven group id org.springframework and artifact id spring-web. 

https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/tree/master/spring-web/src/main/java/org/spr
