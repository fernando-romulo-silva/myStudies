Which of the following are stereotype annotations?

A)
@Bean

B)
@Controller

C)
@Service

D)
@Repository











































Answers 'B', 'C' and 'D' 

Stereotype Annotations are meta-annotations of @Component. 

Bean annotation is used on methods defining a bean inside a @Configuration class.

https://docs.spring.io/spring/docs/current/spring-framework-reference/core.html#beans-stereotype-annotations
