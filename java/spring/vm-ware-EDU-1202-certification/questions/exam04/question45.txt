What are transactions taking place within the same resource (same database) called?

A)
Internal Transactions
​
B)
Confined Transactions
​
C)
Local Transactions

D)​
Scoped Transactions







































Answers 'A' and 'B'

Explanation

Local transactions are resource-specific, such as a transaction associated with a JDBC connection. 

https://docs.spring.io/spring/docs/5.0.7.RELEASE/spring-framework-reference/data-access.html#transaction-motivation
