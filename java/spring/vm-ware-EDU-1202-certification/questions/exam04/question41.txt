Which of the following types of AOP advice may potentially be used to suppress an exception thrown in the advised method?

A)
​@Around

B)​
@Before
​
C)
@AfterReturning
​
D)
@AfterThrowing







































Answers 'A' and 'D'

Explanation

Around and after throwing can both suppress exceptions thrown in the advised exception. 

After returning advice will only be executed if the advised method has not thrown any exception (thus no exception suppression can happen).
