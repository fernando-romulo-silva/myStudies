Which of the following are health indicators provided by Spring Boot Actuator by default?

A)​
ElasticsearchHealthIndicator

B)​
ApplicationHealthIndicator

C)​
ConnectionHealthIndicator

D)​
DiskSpaceHealthIndicator





































Answers 'A' and 'D'

DiskSpaceHealthIndicator - Verifies that available disk space is above a certain threshold. 

ElasticsearchHealthIndicator - Verifies the availability of an Elasticsearch cluster. 

ConnectionHealthIndicator and ApplicationHealthIndicator do not exist. 

https://docs.spring.io/spring-boot/docs/current/reference/htmlsingle/#production-ready-health-indicators
