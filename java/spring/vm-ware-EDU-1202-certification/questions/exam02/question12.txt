In Spring Security ___________ comes after _______________?
  ​
A)
Principal, Authentication
​
B)
Principal, Authorization
​
C)
Authorization, Authentication

D)​
Authentication, Authorization







































Answer 'C'

Authentication is process of validating the user who he claims to be. 

Once the person is authenticated, he is allowed to perform certain actions based on his role, which is authorization.
